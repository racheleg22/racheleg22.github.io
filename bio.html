About

Education and Career Objectives

Hi, I'm Rachel!

I am completing my Masters of Science in Biomedical Engineering at Robert R. McCormick School of Engineering and Applied Science at Northwestern University. My interest in data science guided my coursework at Northwestern. I concentrated in data and software allowing me to gain experience and knowledge in biomedical applications of machine learning and data engineering.

I currently work as a Research Engineer at the Rush Alzheimer's Disease Center, a research center within the Rush University Medical Center. I work within the Devices Team to ensure proper collection and processing of data from wearable devices for mobility research.

I am fascinated by the power of data science to extract information from complex data forms to uncover information with significant implications in health and engineering. I am further interested in the applications of machine learning and integrated technology such as virtual reality and its applications in accessible health care. I am excited to continue to learn in this evolving field through experience and challenges.
